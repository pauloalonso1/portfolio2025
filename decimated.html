UI/UX for a post-apocalyptic
Web3 survival game.

Decimated is a post-apocalyptic web3 survival game set in a dystopian cyberpunk world. Built on Unreal Engine, it merges high-end graphics with blockchain technology — allowing players to explore, trade, and fight across a devastated Earth. Players earn crypto rewards through PvP combat, looting, and missions. With a strong focus on player-driven economy and true digital ownership of assets, Decimated redefines the next era of immersive, decentralized gaming.
