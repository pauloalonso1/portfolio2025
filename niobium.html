(01)

Brand Mark

The Niobium symbol represents advanced technology, continuous innovation, and intelligent solutions. Modern and angular characteristics convey movement and precision. The vibrant green symbolizes growth and innovation, while strong typography reinforces trust and clarity in communications.

(02)

The Spacing

The protective area establishes the clear space and exclusion zone around the logo to maintain visibility and integrity in all applications. The spacing is defined using a 1x proportion derived from the height of the smallest square that can be formed by the internal line thickness of the Niobium symbol.

(03)

Icon

The Niobium icon serves as the visual synthesis of the identity, maintaining strength and legibility across all applications. Optimized for apps, shortcuts, interfaces, and social platforms — it carries the full weight of the brand in its most reduced form.

(04)

Tints & Tones

The color palette distills the brand's personality into two essential forces: Niobium Green — a vibrant lime (#C8FF00) representing energy and technological advancement — and Niobium Gray — a deep charcoal (#212121) representing solidity, authority, and trust.
