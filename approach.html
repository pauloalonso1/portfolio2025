Process

Visual Direction

In the initial phase of visual direction, I shape the project's aesthetic essence and strategic intent. Through deep research, exploration of references, and collaborative dialogue with clients, I define the tone, emotion, and visual logic behind the experience. UI/UX Design

At the core of my design process lies the fusion of usability and emotion. Through user-centered research, interface mapping, and conceptual exploration in tools like Figma, I craft seamless experiences that balance beauty with performance. This phase defines structure, behavior, and interface personality — laying the groundwork for intuitive and engaging digital journeys.

By integrating artificial intelligence into the creative workflow, I accelerate ideation and elevate visual output.
